Appointment

Thank you for signing into the site as you can see by the title you have been sent to the booking page where an appointment can be made.

Important info to note the date is set to month day year therefore input the value accordingly to this structure.

You can also click on the icons inside the buttons for the time/date for an easier selection for each part.

First choose a date thats accessiable for you thats preferably Monday to Friday.

After a date i chosen please select a time that is reasonable and allows you to attend.

Then press book where the values will be stored and you will recieve an email shortly if done correctly.

Now you have an appointment just wait for that date and make sure to attend.
